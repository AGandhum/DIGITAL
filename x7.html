X7

The BMW X7 is a full-size luxury SUV that combines elegance, comfort, and strong petrol performance. It features a spacious, high-tech cabin, adaptive suspension, and premium materials, making it perfect for family travel or long-distance touring.

Powertrain: Inline-6 or V8 — 375–523 hp.

Performance: 0–60 mph ≈ 5.8–4.5 s.

Fuel Efficiency: 30 mpg.

Dimensions: 203″ L × 78″ W × 71″ H.

Price: From $45,000.
